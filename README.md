彌陀淨土系統 | Amitabha Pure Land System
本師釋迦牟尼佛| 行普賢菩薩願 ． 歸彌陀極樂國
這是一個基於《無量壽經》、《華嚴經》與《法華經》教義開發的極簡主義修行法寶。本系統旨在透過數位儀軌，協助現代行者於娑婆世界中預習極樂世界的清淨法性，落實「咸修行普賢之德」的本願。

計畫宗指 | Vision and Purpose
本系統非僅為計數工具，而是一個「境隨心轉」的實修平台。
• 教理基礎： 依據《無量壽經》第二十二願，強調「十念必生」的信願與「廣修萬行」的普賢德。
• 核心精神： 建立在百分之百仰賴彌陀願力的磐石上，透過每日累積資糧，於心中化生七寶池蓮花。
• 美學哲學： 採用極簡向量圖形設計，排除一切多餘視覺干擾，呈現「真空妙有」的法界莊嚴。
修持模組詳解 | System Modules
一、每日定課 ． 動態行持 (Daily Practices)
將抽象教理具象化為可互動的修持流程：
• 三轉法輪 (Three Turnings): 從阿含、般若到唯識，引導行者轉心轉境，調頻心向法界。
• 發菩提心 (Arousing Bodhicitta): 核心十念法模組。透過點亮金蓮的視覺想念，在喧囂中扎根正念。
• 般若智慧 (Prajna Wisdom): 系統化佛法問答法陣，藉由抉擇正見來破除無明。
• 琉璃淨水 (Lapis Lazuli Water): 專注力訓練，象徵洗滌六根塵勞，回歸清淨本然。
• 八十八佛大懺悔文 (88 Buddhas Repentance): 提供語音導讀與儀軌導引，助行者每日至誠懺悔，消業增福。
二、深入經藏 ． 心觀無相 (Deep Studies)
層層遞進的教理探勘系統：
• 九大專欄 (The Nine Columns): 包含「俯瞰華藏」、「唯識之旅」、「法華密碼」等，深度解析大乘經典精髓。
• 彌陀 48 願海 (Amitabha's 48 Vows): 系統終極玄機。需累積滿一萬聲念佛資糧後方能啟動，直擊彌陀本願核心。
三、助道法寶 ． 菩提靜室 (Dharma Supports)
現代生活的數位化城與清淨社群：
• 菩提客棧 (Lotus Forum): 蓮友交流中心。為減法界之熵，採「七日一循環」制，法語七日後自動歸於寂靜，僅留存最新五十篇，確保道場清淨。
• 琉璃多寶閣 (Lapis Pavilion): 護念親友之專屬化城。藉由實修顯化向量寶珠，復於祈福時歸零歸空，成就無相佈施。
• 金蓮解惑 (Golden Lotus Q&A): 針對行者生活困惑，提供隨順法性的叩問與指引。
技術與安全哲學 | Technical and Privacy Philosophy
本系統以「絕對清淨」為開發初衷，技術選型完全符合法界「無所住」之精神：
• 無伺服器架構 (Serverless): 全系統由前端驅動，由 GitHub Pages 託管，無中心化資料庫監控。
• 零知識與隱私 (Zero-Knowledge): 絕不讀取、不傳輸用戶資料。所有資糧數據皆存於行者本地設備，確保絕對隱私。
• 零追蹤 (Zero Tracking): 嚴格禁止廣告、Cookies 或任何形式的商業追蹤腳本。
• 向量美學 (Vector Design): 堅持採用純數學向量圖形（SVG），無位圖、無表情符號，確保程式碼純淨與加載效率。
• PWA 支援: 可直接加入手機主畫面作為單機版 App 使用，隨時隨地開啟數位道場。
修證階位說明 | Progression and Ranking
系統階位反映了行者從「初發心」到「十地位」的轉化歷程：
• 資糧升等: 反映《無量壽經》中「此處修善一日，勝在極樂國為善百歲」之速疾。
• 晉升玄機: 系統中的快速跨越，象徵蒙彌陀願力接引，省下輪迴蹉跎的無數劫時間，直登不退轉。
• 視覺流轉: 圖示隨資糧進階而演化，象徵行者從「心隨境轉」昇華至「境隨心轉」。

🌐 Overview / 總覽
Amitabha Pure Land System | 彌陀淨土系統
Original Teacher Shakyamuni Buddha | Practicing Samantabhadra's Virtues, Returning to Amitabha's Pure Land
This is a minimalist spiritual practice Dharma tool developed based on the teachings of the Infinite Life Sutra, the Avatamsaka Sutra, and the Lotus Sutra. This system aims to assist modern practitioners through digital rituals to prepare for the pure nature of the Pure Land while living in the Saha World, fulfilling the fundamental vow of "all shall practice the virtues of Samantabhadra."
Enter the System | 立即進入道場
Vision and Purpose | 計畫宗旨
This system is not merely a counting tool, but a practical platform for "transforming the environment through the mind."
• Doctrinal Foundation: Based on the 22nd Vow of the Infinite Life Sutra, emphasizing the faith and vow of "rebirth through ten recitations" and the universal virtues of Samantabhadra.
• Core Spirit: Built upon the solid foundation of relying 100% on Amitabha's vow power. By accumulating daily merit, a lotus flower is manifested in the mind's seven-jeweled pond.
• Aesthetic Philosophy: Adopting minimalist vector graphics to eliminate all unnecessary visual interference, presenting the solemnity of the Dharma realm's "true emptiness and wondrous existence."
System Modules | 修持模組詳解
1. Daily Practices & Dynamic Actions
Transforming abstract doctrines into interactive spiritual processes:
• Three Turnings of the Dharma Wheel: Guiding the practitioner from the Agamas, through Prajna, to Yogacara, shifting the mindset and aligning the frequency with the Dharma realm.
• Arousing Bodhicitta: The core ten-recitation module. Anchoring mindfulness amidst worldly noise through the visual contemplation of lighting a golden lotus.
• Prajna Wisdom: A systematic Dharma Q&A matrix, breaking through ignorance by establishing right views.
• Lapis Lazuli Pure Water: Concentration training, symbolizing the cleansing of the six sense faculties and returning to original purity.
• 88 Buddhas Repentance: Provides audio guidance and digital rituals to help practitioners sincerely repent daily, clear karmic obstacles, and increase blessings.
2. Deep Sutra Studies & Formless Contemplation
A progressive textual and doctrinal exploration system:
• The Nine Columns: Includes "Overlooking Hua-yan," "Journey of Yogacara," and "Lotus Sutra Cipher," providing deep analyses of the essence of Mahayana texts.
• Amitabha's 48 Vows Ocean: The ultimate mystery of the system. This module is unlocked only after accumulating 10,000 pure Buddha recitations, striking directly at the core of Amitabha's fundamental vows.
3. Dharma Support Tools & Bodhi Rooms
Digital manifestations and pure communities for modern living:
• Lotus Forum: A community center for lotus friends. To reduce the entropy of the Dharma realm, it operates on a "7-day cycle." All Dharma words automatically return to silence after seven days, keeping only the latest 50 posts to ensure the purity of the Dojo.
• Lapis Treasure Pavilion: An exclusive sanctuary for protecting and blessing family and friends. Practitioners manifest vector-based jewels through practice, then dissolve them into the void during dedication, achieving formless giving.
• Golden Lotus Q&A: Providing Dharma-aligned guidance and answers to the anxieties and confusions of practitioners' daily lives.
Technical and Privacy Philosophy | 技術與安全哲學
This system was developed with "absolute purity" as its original intention. The technology stack perfectly aligns with the Dharma realm's spirit of "non-attachment":
• Serverless Architecture: The entire system is front-end driven and hosted on GitHub Pages, with no centralized database monitoring.
• Zero-Knowledge & Privacy: User data is never read or transmitted. All merit data is stored locally on the practitioner's device, ensuring absolute privacy.
• Zero Tracking: Strict prohibition of advertisements, cookies, or any form of commercial tracking scripts.
• Vector Aesthetics: Strict adherence to pure mathematical vector graphics (SVG). No bitmaps and no emojis are used, ensuring clean code and optimal loading efficiency.
• PWA Enabled: Can be directly added to the mobile home screen as a standalone offline application, opening the digital Dojo anytime, anywhere.
Progression and Ranking | 修證階位說明
The system ranking reflects the practitioner's transformational journey from the "Initial Aspiration" to the "Tenth Bhumi":
• Accelerated Merit: Reflects the teaching in the Infinite Life Sutra that "practicing goodness here for one day surpasses a hundred years of goodness in the Pure Land."
• The Mystery of Advancement: The rapid progression within the system symbolizes receiving Amitabha's vow power, saving the practitioner countless eons of wandering in samsara, and directly reaching the stage of non-retrogression.
• Visual Evolution: Icons evolve as merit advances, symbolizing the practitioner's elevation from "the mind moving with the environment" to "the environment transforming according to the mind."
