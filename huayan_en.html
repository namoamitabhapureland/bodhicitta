Tier 13
Saha & Pure Land

You have touched the core of the Dharma Realm.

Within the infinite expanse of the Avatamsaka World Ocean, both our Saha World and Amitabha's Land of Ultimate Bliss abide in this 13th dimension.

Amitabha's Pure Land is a "waystation" established out of great compassion. By turning the mind inward and first returning to the Pure Land to ensure non-retrogression, one can perfectly fulfill Samantabhadra's vows within this boundless macro-universe.

Avatamsaka Sutra
The Lotus Store World Ocean

The ocean of Dharma is vast, fully comprehended only by the Buddhas. This text is a humble attempt by a lay practitioner to outline the ultimate truth based on sutras and logic. Any errors stem from personal limits. May the Three Jewels guide us.

I. Light of Vairocana

The Avatamsaka Sutra reveals the highest realm of the universe, perceived by Shakyamuni Buddha in deep meditation under the Bodhi tree. It is hailed as the "King of Sutras."

Its core reveals the ultimate reality of "One True Dharma Realm, without hindrance among all phenomena." Ordinary beings consider the visible starry sky as "vast", unaware that visible forms are illusory aggregates; they deem the invisible dust as "microscopic", yet formlessness contains the truth.

"One is all, all is one. A mustard seed contains Mount Sumeru; a single thought is eternity."

The sutra uses the number "10" to symbolize infinite perfection. This boundless universe is entirely the manifestation of the pure mind of Vairocana Buddha (The Great Sun Tathagata). Every speck of dust is a flow of the Tathagata's Dharma body.

II. Twenty Tiers of Jewel Light

Above the void's Great Wind Wheel lies the infinite Fragrant Ocean. Its core gives rise to a Great Lotus of a Thousand Petals, supporting 20 overlapping tiers of World-Seeds:

01. Supreme Light Illuminating
02. Various Fragrant Lotuses
03. All Jewel Adornments
04. Various Bright Flowers
05. Radiating Wondrous Light
06. Pure Wondrous Light
07. Array of Flowers
08. Producing Majestic Light
09. Emitting Wondrous Sounds
10. Vajra Banner
11. Indra Blue Jewel Light
12. Shining Brilliance
13.Saha / Pure Land
14. Peaceful Dustless Light
15. Wondrous Light Lamps
16. Pure Light Illuminating
17. Jewel Adornment
18. Free from Dust
19. Universal Pure Light
20. Wondrous Jewel Flame

Among these 20 dimensions, the most inconceivable is the 13th Tier. Here, Shakyamuni Buddha guides stubborn beings in the Saha world; simultaneously, Amitabha Buddha established the Land of Ultimate Bliss, a universal waystation bridging all realms.

III. The Ultimate Destination

Though the Lotus Store World is vast, it cannot be entered without "vows and practices." The sutra records the youth Sudhana making 53 visits to great spiritual teachers, symbolizing the 53 stages from an ordinary being to Buddhahood.

However, the pinnacle of Avatamsaka is not suspended in the void, but has a true destination! The two greatest Bodhisattvas, Manjushri and Samantabhadra, both seamlessly directed the ultimate fruit of Avatamsaka to Amitabha's Pure Land:

"When my life approaches its end, may all obstacles be destroyed.
Seeing Amitabha Buddha, may I be born in the Land of Peace and Joy."
— Samantabhadra's Vows

IV. Closing the Loop

The crown jewel of the sutra is "Samantabhadra's Ten Great Vows." But where are these endless vows actualized? The teaching of Shakyamuni Buddha is consistent from beginning to end. The answer is etched in Amitabha's 22nd Vow:

"Those born in my land will ultimately reach the Stage of One Lifetime to Buddhahood... They will all cultivate the virtues of Samantabhadra."

The Saha world holds too many retrogressive conditions, making it extremely hard to enter the Avatamsaka realm. Thus, Amitabha's great compassion created the Pure Land as a "Waystation of the Bodhisattva Path." Once born there, one attains non-retrogression and flawless spiritual power, effortlessly fulfilling Samantabhadra's vast vows.

This digital dojo is established to help practitioners preview the Pure Land. One session of mindful recitation aligns you with the 13th Tier; continuous mindfulness is walking Sudhana's path of Bodhi.
