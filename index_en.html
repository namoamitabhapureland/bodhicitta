【 Mission 】
Following Shakyamuni Buddha
Practicing Samantabhadra's Vows
Returning to Amitabha's Pure Land
Q: Why design this digital dojo?
According to the 22nd vow of the Infinite Life Sutra, "All should cultivate the virtues of Samantabhadra." Our diligent accumulation of merit is not a trap of "self-power," but is built on the foundation of "100% unwavering faith in Amitabha's vow of reception."

The moment a practitioner generates Bodhicitta and vows to be born in the Pure Land, a dedicated lotus blossoms for them in Amitabha's seven-jewel pond. Accumulating merit persistently is preparation for practice in the Pure Land.

The Pure Land is truly an "intermediate station on the Bodhisattva path." This tool helps practitioners preview the great practices of Samantabhadra in the Saha world, adapting early to the pure nature where "the environment transforms according to the mind."
Q: Saha vs. Pure Land Time?
The Infinite Life Sutra states: "Practicing goodness here for ten days and nights surpasses practicing goodness in other Buddha lands for a thousand years."

【 Deep Meaning 】 Practicing in this world of five turbidities accumulates merit most rapidly. A single pure thought, every Buddha's name recited, is true, flawless merit.
Q: Meaning of Ten Recitations?
Centered on the "Ten Recitations Method." Every completion embodies Faith, Vows, and Practice. The rise in merit is not blind counting, but a true measure of mental transformation and the cleansing of defilements.
Q: Significance of ranks and visual changes?
Integrating the Avatamsaka, Lotus, and Pure Land sutras. The visual changes as merit accumulates symbolize elevating from "mind swayed by environment" to "environment transformed by mind," allowing intuitive perception of one's pure nature.
Q: Does promotion speed correspond to the Three Asamkhyeya Kalpas?
The accumulated "days" are not physical time, but a "manifest indicator of spiritual merit."

In the Saha world, initiating practice is extremely difficult, hence every persistent step yields exponential growth in the Dharma realm. Traditionally, reaching the 8th Bhumi takes an Asamkhyeya Kalpa, but the Pure Land path is "inconceivable." The system's rapid leaps symbolize Amitabha's vows saving you countless eons of cyclic existence.

【 Path to Buddhahood 】 However, even after attaining non-retrogression in the Pure Land, one still awaits the Buddhas' prophecy and must traverse countless eons saving beings to achieve supreme Buddhahood. This is the profound mechanism behind the system's infinite ranking design.
Q: How does the system interact with me?
The Dharma treasure has a spirit; it responds to sincerity. The visual transformations respond profoundly to the practitioner's persistent vows. Like drinking water, one knows for oneself whether it is cold or warm; this realm of realization is left for you to experience.

【 Continuous Support 】 This digital dojo will be continuously maintained and upgraded according to karmic conditions, guarding the practitioner's Bodhi mind.
Q: Why a Web App and not a traditional App?
To maintain the dojo's "absolute purity" and "privacy."

Traditional apps require permissions or external servers. This tool uses pure frontend technology, with no server tracking and no database storage. All your data lives "100%" on your personal device, with zero risk of leaks.
