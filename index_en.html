Dedication of Merit

May the merit and virtue accrued from this work,
Adorn the Buddha's Pure Land,
Repay the four great kindnesses above,
And relieve the suffering of those in the three paths below.
May those who see and hear of this,
All bring forth the Bodhi resolve.
When this retribution body is over,
May we be born together in the Land of Ultimate Bliss.
