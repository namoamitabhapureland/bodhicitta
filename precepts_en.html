Altar Guide
【 Dual-Track Altar 】
Practitioners may choose one track based on their conditions. Whether it is the fasting of the 8 Precepts or the mind-guarding of the Bodhisattva Vows, all merits can be dedicated to the Pure Land.
【 Monthly Limit ． 6 Times 】
Following the principle of the Six Uposatha days, the altar is limited to 6 times per month to prevent attachment. If reached, please wait for the next month.

Upon completion of the 24-hour period, the system will award 500 points.
