The Lotus Sutra speaks of the "Phantom City". The path to the Pure Land is long and arduous. Out of compassion for weary beings, the Buddha conjured a city for temporary rest.

This Pavilion is that worldly phantom city. The manifestations herein are skillful means to guide practitioners in making "Specific Dedications" to protect loved ones.
Please do not give rise to worldly attachments of collection or greed. As forms dissolve and vows arise, each reset is a practice of formless giving.
[ Laws of Resonance ]
1. Hidden Guides: Tap an unmanifested "Empty Niche" to reveal its formula.
2. Vow Resonance: Selecting a possessed manifestation will cause its matching pair to glow.
3. Steps of Ascension: Two basic forms merge into an advanced form; two advanced forms resonate into the ultimate mandala.
